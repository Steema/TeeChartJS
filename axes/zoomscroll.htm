Default behavior is:
  • Zoom by dragging left mouse button from top-left to right-bottom.
  • Zoom can also be done by dragging the mouse wheel, if enabled.
  • Reset zoom (unzoom) by dragging left mouse button from right-bottom to top-left.
  • Scroll by dragging right mouse button.
