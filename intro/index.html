TeeChart for Javascript supports 14 basic data-series types that may be combined as required. Display options include charts, maps and gauges.
Line series can include points. The Line width is configurable, may be smoothed and can include permanent mark-labels. This example uses mouseover hints to show point data.
Bar series may be vertical or horizontal. For financial applications a Volume visualisation is provided that combines very well with Candle (OHLC) data.
This Point (scatter) chart shows data of an empirical nature. The line has been applied to show averages along the x range.
Areas may be transparent and can be smoothed and stacked.
Pie chart data may also be shown as a donut or as several concentric rings.
Gantt bars may be used for project planning. The bars on this may be drag-moved and resized.
Large datasets may be navigated via the use of a scroller. Drag the slider on the bottom part of the chart to see different parts of the total data.
